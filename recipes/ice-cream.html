Ice Cream

Description

This shit is pretty bomb.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cream, and 1/2 cup of sugar to a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Meanwhile, combine egg yolks wiht remaining 1/4 cup sugar until mixed
  3. Once sugar is disolved in milk mixture, slowly add egg mixture
  4. Stir and turn off heat at 170F or until some eggs start to scramble
  5. Add vanilla, salt, and any other flavoring
  6. Regrigerate for 4 hours or overnight
  7. Strain and mix in ice cream maker for 25 minutes
  8. Add goodies and mix for 5 more minutes
  9. Transfer ice cream to closed container and freeze for 4 hours or overnight
  10. Enjoy!
